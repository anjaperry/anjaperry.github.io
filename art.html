Art Projects

Project 1: Dúlachán

This art piece was a concept art made surrounding the Irish mythological being known as a Dúlachán. They were the original inspiration for the Headless Horseman and I wanted to create a piece for it. The inspiration for the piece was a photograph I took while I was living in Scotland.


Project 2: Chāoxīng Zhúlóng

This is an art piece of a character I made for a Dungeons and Dragons campaign. Much of my art is inspired by the characters that I and my friends create for our campaigns. They are an Eladrin Four Elements Monk inspired by Chinese mythology and constellations of the Four Guardians.


Project 3: Bear

This is an art piece of a character I made for a Dungeons and Dragons campaign. They are a Shifter Totem Warrior Barbarian inspired by a joke about being a bear barbarian. I love making funny little bits for my characters.


Project 4: The Misadventures of Interworld 42

This is an art piece of a character I made for a Dungeons and Dragons campaign. This is the entire group together consisting of a Tiefling Bard, Genasi Paladin, Half Orc Paladin, Human Fighter, and a Kobold Rogue. This was the piece that I made for the group to celebrate the end of our campaign.
